A King's Angel

When: November 3 - November 18

Where: The Parachute Factory

Who: Alec Ganesha

Alec Ganesha is a self-taught mix media pop artist. This is his premiere exhibition, A Kings Angel at the Parachute Factory. He specializes in mixed-media style pop art, with 24K gold leaf and vibrant colors to pay homage to his parent's heritage and culture. He applies a high gloss epoxy resin to enhance the paintings, as you have never seen before.

Alec draws inspiration from the tragedy of losing his mother too soon. He incorporates different life experiences that he had shared with his mom during the brief time they spent together, into all of his work.
